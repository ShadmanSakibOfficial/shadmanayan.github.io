ABOUT ME

Assalamu Alaikum

I’m Shadman Sakib Ayan. At first, I am a Muslim. My country's name is Bangladesh. I live in Lohagara Upazila of Narail district in Bangladesh. I did my primary and high school here. My primary school name is Lohagara Government Primary School. And the name of my high school is Lohagara Government Pilot High School. From here I passed SSC in 2017. After my SSC exam, I shifted from Lohagara to Jessore Bangladesh in 2017. There I was admitted to college. My college name is BAF Shaheen College, Jashore. And from here in 2019 I completed my HSC. My SSC and HSC background was science. After my HSC exam, I shifted from Jessore to Dhaka. I was admitted to the CSE department of the ECE faculty of North South University in the spring season of 2020. And at the moment I am doing a BS.c in Computer Science Engineering (CSE) here. I have a beautiful family. My family consists of my father, mother, elder sister, and me. We are 2 siblings. My sister is older and I am younger. My father is a doctor. My mother is a housewife and my sister is a doctor. I also have another person in my family. He is our most cherished pet cat. His name is Pew. One of the best things about my life is Pew. I love him so much. I have some hobbies besides studying. I like to play the guitar. When I was in class 9, my dad bought me a guitar. That’s name was Hobner acoustic guitar. From then on I started learning guitar. In 3 months I learned all the basics of guitar. Then I went up to the division stage in the guitar competition. In 2019, I first received an invitation to play the guitar at a big guitar show in America. Then I do guitar collaborations with some of the biggest musicians in the country and some musicians from outside the country. I have played guitar in several bands. I usually like to play heavy metal, rock, thrash metal songs. Another hobby I enjoy is playing with cats. I like cats very much. Also, I like to tour. Although never going anywhere far away. I like adventure-type things.
